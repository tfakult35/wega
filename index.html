Who are we?

We have long become apathethic to those whose hearts are closed to the Truth, on whom the Spirit of the Truth can not perch.

> On this site you can find the synthesized research of ours and of our predecessors. We understand that the Truth, no matter the dreadful feelings it induces within a man, will always be freeing, purifying, deifying. Let this resource be a blazing hearth for you in this misty cold barren world.
